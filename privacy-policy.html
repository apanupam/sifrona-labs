Privacy Policy – Sifrona Labs

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Such as your name, email, phone number, and business information when you contact us or fill out forms.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website (pages visited, device type, browser version, etc.).
  • Cookies: Used to enhance your browsing experience, analytics, and website functionality.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Communicate project updates, support, and new offerings.
  • Analyze performance and improve user experience.
  • Ensure platform security and compliance with applicable laws.
3. Data Security

We use secure servers, encryption, and access controls to protect your data. While we follow industry standards, no digital system is entirely risk-free, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.
Information may only be shared with trusted service providers or partners who assist in delivering our services — under strict confidentiality agreements — or when required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of promotional messages.
  • Disable cookies via your browser settings.

For assistance, contact us anytime.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to other websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party sites.

7. Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The latest version will always be available on our website.
